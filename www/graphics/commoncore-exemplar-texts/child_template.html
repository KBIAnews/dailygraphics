Examples Of Common Core Suggested ‘Exemplar Texts’

Grades K-1
Green Eggs and Ham by Dr. Seuss
Amazing Whales! by Sarah L. Thomson
Grades 2-3
Sarah, Plain and Tall by Patricia MacLachlan
“Stopping by Woods on a Snowy Evening” by Robert Frost
Grades 4–5
“Zlateh the Goat” by Isaac Bashevis Singer
Hurricanes: Earth’s Mightiest Storms by Patricia Lauber
Grades 6–8
Narrative of the Life of Frederick Douglass, an American Slave, Written by Himself by Frederick Douglass
Little Women by Louisa May Alcott
Grades 9–10
Things Fall Apart by Chinua Achebe
“Sonnet 73” by William Shakespeare
Recommended Levels of Insulation by U.S. Environmental Protection Agency/U.S. Department of Energy
Grades 11-12
The Tipping Point: How Little Things Can Make a Big Difference by Malcolm Gladwell
Walden by Henry David Thoreau

Source: Common Core State Standards for English Language Arts & Literacy in History/Social Studies, Science, and Technical Subjects — Appendix B: Text Exemplars and Sample Performance Tasks (PDF)
