Organizations Directly Connected To Peterson

Committee for a Responsible Federal Budget

Founded 1981 | Mission is public education on issues with "significant fiscal policy impact"

Moment of Truth

2011 | Grassroots effort in aftermath of Simpson-Bowles Commission

Campaign to Fix the Debt

Launched 2012 | Has promoted "grand bargain" to cut deficit and debt

Related: Committee for a Responsible Federal Budget Fix the Debt Coalition The Can Kicks Back Comeback America Initiative

The Can Kicks Back

Launched 2012 | Working with Campaign to Fix the Debt to mobilize millennials for "sustainable and generationally equitable" budget. Advisory board includes Erskine Bowles Alan Simpson David Walker

Fix the Debt Coalition

Founded 2012 | Lobbying and advocacy arm of Campaign to Fix the Debt

Concord Coalition

Founded 1992 | Educational organization focused on budget deficits and "unsustainable entitlement programs." Peterson is a co-founder.

"Fiscal Wake-Up" and "Fiscal Solutions" tours

2010-11 | Grassroots events and town hall meetings to promote goals of Simpson-Bowles and Peterson-Pew commissions

"I.O.U.S.A"

2009 | Documentary film covering Concord Coalition issues and its Fiscal Wake-Up Tour

Peter G. Peterson Foundation

Founded 2008

Projects and Related Organizations

Peterson-Pew Commission on Budget Reform

2009-11 | Created to develop repair plan for federal budget process, in partnership with Pew Charitable Trusts

"Indebted"

2009-14 | Media campaign with Viacom-owned mtvU campus TV network; includes online video game "Debt Ski"

Annual Fiscal Summits

2010-14 | Peterson Foundation's "signature event," bringing together national leaders and experts

OweNo

2010-11 | Grassroots and advertising campaign on "long-term fiscal challenges"

Coalition for Fiscal and National Security

2012 | Former defense, economic and foreign-policy officials labelling the national debt as "the single greatest threat to our national security"

Fiscal Confidence Index

2012 | Monthly surveys to measure public concern over national debt

I'm Ready!

2013 | Contest for grassroots videos promoting awareness of national debt

Cost of Crisis-Driven Fiscal Policy
